About the Arroyo Cult Arts Board

Welcome to the Arroyo Cult Arts Board web page! We are dedicated to preserving and promoting the unique arts and cultural heritage of our desert community. We host events, exhibitions, and workshops celebrating this heritage.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for artistic expression, to foster appreciation for our region's unique art traditions, and enrich the community through art. We strive to be a meeting place for artists and enthusiasts alike. Join us in keeping this heritage alive!
