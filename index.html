About the Arroyo Cult Arts Board

Welcome to the Arroyo Cult Arts Board web page! We are dedicated to promoting the unique contemporary arts and preserving the cultural heritage of our desert community. We host events, exhibitions, and workshops celebrating this heritage.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for contemporary artistic expression, to foster appreciation for our region's unique art traditions, and enrich the community through art. We strive to be a meeting place for artists and enthusiasts alike. Join us in keeping the arts alive!
